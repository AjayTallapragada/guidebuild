# Final Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1: The worker persona
- Primary persona: app-based delivery rider operating 5-6 days per week in urban corridors.
- Pain: lost wages from rain, traffic gridlock, and road incidents.
- Promise: weekly parametric protection that pays fast without paperwork.

## Slide 2: Why ParcelShield AI
- Policies tuned for delivery-specific disruptions.
- AI evaluates triggers and fraud risk instantly.
- Workers receive simulated wage replacement through familiar payout rails.

## Slide 3: Product flow
- Buy weekly cover.
- Detect disruption through manual or automated signals.
- Score fraud and compare weather claims with historical baselines.
- Auto-approve low-risk claims.
- Release payout through Razorpay/Stripe/UPI simulator.

## Slide 4: AI and fraud architecture
- Trigger engine: weather, delay, and accident adapters.
- Fraud signals: GPS drift, speed outliers, proof availability, historical weather mismatch.
- Decisioning: `approve_fast`, `review`, `reject`.
- Audit trail captures all payout and decision events.

## Slide 5: Dashboard intelligence
- Worker dashboard: earnings protected, active weekly coverage, recent AI decisions.
- Admin dashboard: loss ratio, flagged claims, seven-day fraud trend, next-week prediction.

## Slide 6: Weekly pricing model
- Weekly premium presented as prepaid protection.
- Coverage priced by risk type, region, and delivery profile.
- Portfolio view helps insurers tune pricing by dominant trigger patterns.

## Slide 7: Business viability
- Low-touch claims handling lowers operational cost.
- Faster trust-building with workers improves retention.
- Fraud controls protect underwriting margins.

## Slide 8: Ask / next steps
- Integrate live weather and map partners.
- Add real payout gateway credentials.
- Expand predictive pricing and zone-level underwriting.
